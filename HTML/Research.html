OUR RESEARCH INITIATIVES

Our research was initiated with a main motive of getting the idea of designing different pages of e-commerce website. At first, we were stucked at a moment as we had no any proper idea of designing any kind of website. So, We did research in different platform and generate multiple ideas from varities of e-commerce website that sells footwear. We, as a team, gain the technique and implemented in our, StepSphere accordingly. We were benefitted by the techniques of implementing technology in designing attractive website. Furthermore, the research helped us to get insights of the best practice and trends in footwear industry. We were aimed to showcase the best products effectively and enhance the experience of user in footwear selection.
